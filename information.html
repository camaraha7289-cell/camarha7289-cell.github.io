Nintendo

The Super Mario Galaxy Movie is an upcoming animated adventure comedy film based on the 2007 video game Super Mario Galaxy and its 2010 sequel, as well as Nintendo's broader Mario franchise. Produced by Illumination and Nintendo, it is the sequel to The Super Mario Bros. Movie (2023). Directed by Aaron Horvath and Michael Jelenic and written by Matthew Fogel, the film stars an ensemble voice cast consisting of Chris Pratt, Anya Taylor-Joy, Charlie Day, Jack Black, and Keegan-Michael Key reprising their roles from the first film, with Benny Safdie, Donald Glover, and Brie Larson joining the cast. In the film, Mario and Luigi, and their friends adventure into outer space where they meet Princess Rosalina and face off against Bowser and his son, Bowser Jr.
